Understanding Student Mental Health

Mental health challenges among students have become increasingly prevalent in today's academic environment.

Depression & Anxiety affect 30% of students
25% experience significant academic stress
20% report regular sleep issues
15% struggle with social isolation
10% face self-esteem challenges

Early recognition and support can significantly improve outcomes. If you're experiencing any of these symptoms, remember you're not alone.
